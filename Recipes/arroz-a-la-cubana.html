Delicous Arroz a la Cubana

Authentic Arroz a la Cubana Recipe

Embark on a culinary journey to the vibrant flavors of Cuba with our Authentic Arroz a la Cubana. This iconic dish is a celebration of simplicity and bold taste, featuring a bed of fluffy white rice adorned with seasoned ground beef, sweet plantains, and a perfectly fried egg. The ground beef is sautéed with a medley of aromatic spices, creating a savory and well-balanced flavor profile. Nestled alongside are the caramelized sweet plantains, adding a delightful touch of sweetness to each bite. Crowned with a golden, runny-yolked fried egg, this dish achieves a harmonious blend of textures and tastes that captures the essence of Cuban home cooking. Elevate your dining experience with this classic Arroz a la Cubana recipe, a symphony of Cuban flavors that's both comforting and unforgettable.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 pound (about 450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 ripe plantains, peeled and sliced diagonally
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped

Steps:

  1. White Rice:
    2. Rinse rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Combine rice, water, and salt in a pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until rice is cooked.
  2. Seasoned Ground Beef:
    3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and red bell pepper. Sauté until vegetables are softened. Stir in crushed tomatoes, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Simmer until the flavors meld.
  3. Sweet Plantains:
    4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry plantain slices until golden brown on each side. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  4. Fried Eggs:
    5. In a separate pan, fry eggs according to your preference. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Assembling:
    6. Serve a portion of seasoned ground beef over a bed of white rice. Add fried sweet plantains on the side. Top each serving with a fried egg. Garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley if desired.

Enjoy the delightful combination of flavors and textures in this Authentic Arroz a la Cubana!
