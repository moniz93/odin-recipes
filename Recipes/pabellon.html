Authentic Venezuelan Pabellón Criollo Recipe

Embark on a culinary journey with our authentic Venezuelan Pabellón Criollo, a dish that captures the essence of Venezuela's rich gastronomic heritage. This classic recipe brings together tender shredded beef, black beans, rice, and sweet plantains in a harmonious medley of flavors and textures. The succulent shredded beef is slow-cooked to perfection, infused with a blend of spices that evoke the warmth of Latin American kitchens. Nestled beside it, the black beans simmered to a velvety consistency, offering a savory counterpart to the sweet and caramelized notes of the ripe plantains. The ensemble is completed with a mound of fluffy white rice, creating a plate that not only delights the palate but also tells a story of tradition and cultural richness. Elevate your dining experience with the vibrant and heartwarming flavors of our Authentic Venezuelan Pabellón Criollo – a celebration on every plate.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds (about 1 kg) beef flank or skirt steak
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups black beans (canned or cooked from dried)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 ripe plantains, peeled and sliced diagonally
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped
  • Avocado slices

Steps:

  1. Shredded Beef:
    1. Season the beef with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
    2. In a large pot, brown the beef on all sides.
    3. Add onions, bell peppers, garlic, and beef broth.
    4. Simmer on low heat until the beef is tender and can be easily shredded.
  2. Black Beans:
    1. In a separate pot, sauté onions, bell peppers, and garlic until softened.
    2. Add black beans, cumin, and salt. Simmer until flavors meld.
  3. White Rice:
    1. Rinse rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    2. Combine rice, water, and salt in a pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until rice is cooked.
  4. Fried Sweet Plantains:
    1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Fry plantain slices until golden brown on each side. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  5. Assembling:
    1. Serve a portion of shredded beef, black beans, white rice, and fried sweet plantains on each plate.
    2. Optionally, garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley and avocado slices.

Now, Enjoy the delightful fusion of flavors and textures in this Authentic Venezuelan Pabellón Criollo!
