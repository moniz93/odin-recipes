Authentic Arroz a la Cubana Recipe

Embark on a culinary journey to the vibrant flavors of Cuba with our Authentic Arroz a la Cubana. This iconic dish is a celebration of simplicity and bold taste, featuring a bed of fluffy white rice adorned with seasoned ground beef, sweet plantains, and a perfectly fried egg. The ground beef is sautéed with a medley of aromatic spices, creating a savory and well-balanced flavor profile. Nestled alongside are the caramelized sweet plantains, adding a delightful touch of sweetness to each bite. Crowned with a golden, runny-yolked fried egg, this dish achieves a harmonious blend of textures and tastes that captures the essence of Cuban home cooking. Elevate your dining experience with this classic Arroz a la Cubana recipe, a symphony of Cuban flavors that's both comforting and unforgettable.
