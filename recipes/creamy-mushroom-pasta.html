Creamy Mushroom Pasta

This Creamy Mushroom Pasta speaks for itself. A slippery tangle of pasta bathed in a creamy parmesan sauce loaded with buttery garlic mushrooms, it’s simple, quick, and utterly luxurious!

Ingredients:

  • 200 g (about 7 oz) of pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • Grated cheese (Parmesan or your favorite)

How to make Creamy Mushroom Pasta:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, sauté the onion and garlic with some oil or butter.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their juices.
  4. Pour in the cream, season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Mix with the cooked pasta and top with grated cheese.
