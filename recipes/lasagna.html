Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of flat, wide pasta sheets alternated with savory fillings and baked until bubbly and golden. The fillings typically include a meat sauce (ragù), béchamel sauce, cheeses like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, and seasonings. It's a comforting and versatile dish, with regional variations in the specific ingredients and sauces used.

ingredients:

  • Meat Sauce (Ragù)
  • Bechamel
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Seasonings
